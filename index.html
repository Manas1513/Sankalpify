Data Structures & Algorithms

Why Learn DSA?

DSA is the bedrock of computer science. Mastering it enables you to write efficient, optimized code and is a primary requirement for passing technical interviews at top tech companies like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft. It teaches you to think like a programmer and solve complex problems systematically.

What You'll Learn (1-Month Syllabus)

  • Week 1: Intro to Complexity, Arrays, Linked Lists, Stacks & Queues.
  • Week 2: Advanced DSA - Trees (Binary, BST), Heaps, Hashing.
  • Week 3: Graph Algorithms (BFS, DFS), Sorting & Searching. Minor Project development.
  • Week 4: Dynamic Programming, Greedy Algorithms, and Major Project implementation.
